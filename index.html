Why I Built This

I got tired of all the nonsense.
I bet you did too.

Look, I'm not the only one who's tried a million wine cellar management apps. They all seem to follow the same magic list of antipatterns:

  • Steep up-front purchases, with no guarantee the app does anything you actually want
  • Cloud backing of questionable quality, longevity, and stability
  • Constant attempts to monetize you further: ads, social features, "opportunities to purchase," suggested drinks, who knows what else

If you're like me, you already know why you bought what you bought. You don't need suggestions on what else to buy. You just have a lot of wine and other stuff to keep track of, and you want to know what you have, how much of it, and where it is.

I built this for myself, because I just wanted something that worked. I fixed all the things I hated about the other apps in my workflow design here. I didn't build features I never used.

Is it perfect? No. Will it keep evolving, getting better as I discover new things to be mad at? Definitely.

We Have Wine at Home! stores your data locally on your device and optionally syncs it across all your devices through iCloud — your iCloud, not mine. Easy CSV import and export means you're always in control of your own data.

The cellar manager lets you define your actual physical cellars, customize shelves and slot counts, and visually place bottles exactly where they live. Overflowing into boxes, the guest room bathtub, or your office mini-fridge? That's fine — bottle placement is as flexible as your collection demands.


🎉 Free. No ads. No subscriptions. No in-app purchases. I built this for myself. If it helps you too, I'm super stoked to hear it.

Everything You Need

Serious tools for serious connoisseurs.

From a quick swipe to mark a bottle consumed to simple bottle logging, filter and sort options, every feature is built for how you actually enjoy your collection.

📦

Parent Records, Individual Bottles

One entry for "2007 Talisman Red Dog Pinot Noir" can represent six (or more!) bottles — each with its own location, price, acquisition date, and consumed status.

📍

Cellar Location Tracking

Use free-text location names with autocomplete, or assign bottles to exact cellar slots. Always know where every bottle lives.

🔍

Powerful Search & Filter

Search across any field, for any bottle type. Apply filters to narrow your browsing experience, or apply a sort to find the oldest first, etc.

👆

Swipe to Consume

Swipe left on any row to mark a bottle consumed in one gesture. Undo anytime.

📸

Label Photos

Photograph labels at the moment of purchase or selection. Photos are saved to a dedicated "Wine Labels" album in your iCloud Photo Library and shown as thumbnails throughout the app.

📥

Import & Export CSV

Bulk-load your existing inventory from a spreadsheet, or export everything as a CSV to share. The format is fully round-trippable (except for images). You control your data.

Visual Cellar Management

See your entire cellar at a glance.

Switch to Cellar View for a color-coded visual grid of every slot. Red, white, rosé, orange, sparkling, dessert — each color has its own shade so you can spot exactly what you want at a glance.

  • Create multiple named cellars — "Front Room", "Garage Fridge", and more
  • Configure each cellar with custom shelves and slot counts
  • Tap any slot to assign or view a bottle
  • Block slots for broad-shouldered bottles
  • Add a new wine and place it directly from any empty slot
  • Mark consumed or remove from the cellar right from the slot view

Your Data, Your Business

Privacy isn't a feature.

We Have Wine at Home! has no accounts, no ads, no backend, no analytics, and no tracking. Your collection data stays entirely on your device and in your personal iCloud account.

🔒

No Accounts Required

Open the app and start adding bottles. No sign-up, no email, no password.

☁️

Data syncs automatically across your iPhone and iPad through your own iCloud account. We track and recieve nothing.

📵

No Analytics

Zero tracking, zero telemetry. We never see what you drink, what you buy, or how you use the app.

📶

Works Offline

Full functionality without an internet connection. Syncs when connectivity is restored.
